Hi everyone, this is Preetam also known as 'chaos' on social media platforms. I'm a 20 year old boy from Jamshedpur, India who has passion in video editing. Since my childhood days I've loved making art although my illustrations skills are still lacking a lot. I had to join an University to study management after completing my school in 2020. Later, I realized that I'm not doing what I love and it could make me regret my decisions in the future. So, I dropped out of my University and started Designing as a Freelancer. Fortunately, people started noticing me and it built my confidence. I somehow managed to get a job as a Graphic & Motion Designer in a good Indian Advertising Agency. Now, I can proudly tell that I've worked with some of the biggest and renowned Brands, Celebrities, Teams and Influencers across the world. Although, I'm not going to quit my studies, I will rejoin a Design School or University in 2022 and continue to learn more about Design & Art. I'm really thankful to everyone who supported and believed in me on taking such a decision in my life.
